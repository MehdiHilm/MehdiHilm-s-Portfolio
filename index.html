Greetings! I'm Mehdi Hilm, a 21-year-old enthusiast from Morocco. My journey is one of perpetual exploration and growth, driven by an insatiable hunger for knowledge and experience. Following my baccalauréat, I delved into economics at the Faculty of Legal, Economic and Social Sciences. However, my quest for versatility led me to shift gears towards construction management, where I honed my skills as a site manager at OFPPT Marrakech. Eager to embrace new challenges, I ventured to Turkey to pursue computer engineering at Kütahya Dumlupınar University. Though the path was fraught with obstacles, each hurdle served as a catalyst for personal and professional development. Returning to Morocco, I found my passion in full stack development at Racine School, where I continue to refine my craft and expand my horizons. My journey is a testament to resilience, adaptability, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. With every twist and turn, I emerge stronger and more determined than before. In addition to my academic pursuits, I've garnered a substantial following as a gaming content creator, boasting over 300k subscribers. Recognized as one of the best gaming content creators in Morocco, I leverage my creativity and expertise to engage audiences worldwide. Join me as we navigate the ever-changing landscape of technology and innovation. Together, let's push boundaries, defy expectations, and chart a course towards success. Let's embark on this journey, together.
